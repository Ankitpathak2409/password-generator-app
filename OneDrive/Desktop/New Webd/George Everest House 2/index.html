About George Everest House

George Everest Peak, near Mussoorie, is a historical gem amidst the Himalayas. Named after Sir George Everest, the former Surveyor-General of India, this peak holds a captivating tale.At 6,578 feet above sea level, Sir George Everest's house, now in ruins, offers breathtaking views of the Doon valley and Aglar river. Recognized by the Uttarakhand government in 2020, plans are underway to transform it into a cartography museum, preserving Sir George's legacy.
